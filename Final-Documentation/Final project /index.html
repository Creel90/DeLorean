About These Stars

Sirius, the brightest star in the night sky, is 8.6 light-years away in the constellation Canis Major.

Betelgeuse, a red supergiant in Orion, is approximately 642 light-years away and known for its massive size.

Vega, in the constellation Lyra, is 25 light-years away and one of the brightest stars visible.
